What I like in an organization

I love an organization where thoughtfulness radiates at every level. I like to see this balanced with a willingness to act and decide, extreme ownership of failure (especially amongst the most senior leaders) and enthusiastic delegation and empowerment of team members. I am and love to see servant leaders who act with and expect their teams to act with integrity.
With my deep history of support for product teams of all sizes, product market fit is something I live and breath. Your company should have it or be actively in pursuit of it. If you haven't found it yet and need support, this is an area I can specifically help with.

What I like in a technical team

I love a team that gives a damn. About the business, the product they are building, about the quality of their code, and about each other. I love to work with brilliant humans with no ego. I love to see technologists be good humans towards each another, great collaborators for their product partners, and pragmatic but courageous in their technical decisions. I love to see technical decisions paired appropriately with company stage and product maturity. I get excited to hear brilliant individuals describe their solutions in terms of its strengths AND weaknesses and look for feedback on mitigating downsides. Which brings me to feedback. I love a team that is good at feedback -- that seeks it out.
